SecurePass-TypeAuthn

SecurePass-TypeAuthn analyzes the rhythm of your typing to build a private behavioral biometric profile. Every key press and pause becomes part of a transparent, secure authentication story.

Get started below by first creating your profile and then testing the authentication experience. Each area explains what to expect so you can type naturally and confidently.

Enroll

Record three guided typing samples so the model can learn your dwell, flight, and pause patterns. We'll only keep anonymized timing data on your device.

Authenticate

Verify your identity with a short phrase challenge, review the confidence score, and unlock your time-based one-time password when the match succeeds.
